I am a Master & PhD student at the [State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Intelligence](http://cogskl.iflytek.com/), [School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science](https://saids.ustc.edu.cn/), University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), supervised by [Prof. Qi Liu](http://staff.ustc.edu.cn/~qiliuql/).

My research interests include **Structured Data Mining**, **Multimodal Generation and Understanding**, **Large Language Models**, and **Knowledge Transfer & Cross-domain Learning**.
